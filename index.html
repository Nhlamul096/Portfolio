About Me

Hi, I’m Nhlamulo Mashele, a data scientist with a keen interest in predictive modeling. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Computational and Applied Mathematics & Economic Science from the University of the Witwatersrand, complemented by my experience in software engineering. My passion lies in leveraging data to forecast trends and inform strategic decisions. I named my website The Future because working with data science feels like time traveling to me; it enables us to glimpse into what lies ahead and make informed choices today.

I thrive in working with large datasets to uncover valuable insights that can shape the future of businesses. Feel free to explore my portfolio and connect with me to discuss how we can collaborate to shape a brighter future through data driven strategies.
